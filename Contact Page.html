Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour package?
Booking a tour package is easy! Simply browse our available packages, select the one that interests you, choose your preferred dates, and follow the booking instructions. You can book directly online or contact our customer service team for assistance with the booking process.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept various payment methods including major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard), Phone Pe, Google Pay, bank transfers, and in some locations, payment through digital wallets. All payments are processed securely to ensure your financial information is protected.
What is your cancellation policy?
Our cancellation policy varies depending on the type of booking and the timing of cancellation. Generally, cancellations made 30 days or more before the scheduled departure receive a full refund minus processing fees. Cancellations made 15-29 days before departure receive a 50% refund, and cancellations made less than 15 days before departure are non-refundable. We recommend purchasing travel insurance for added protection.
Do you offer travel insurance?
Yes, we offer comprehensive travel insurance options that cover trip cancellation, medical emergencies, baggage loss, and other unforeseen circumstances. We highly recommend purchasing travel insurance for all trips, especially international journeys. Our travel consultants can provide you with different insurance options tailored to your specific travel needs.
